    Rules

    Select your unit, move to highlighted cells, then attack enemies in range. Use cards smartly.
    • Defeat the enemy Leader to win.
    • Pandav morale: +1 damage within 2 tiles of your Leader.
    • Kaurav fear: enemy Pandav units beside the Kaurav Leader lose 1 move.
    • Rivers: only Cavalry and Chariots may occupy; melee in a river is weaker.
    • Mud slows movement from that tile; wind and mud random events tweak damage.
    • Momentum: chain attacks or survive hits — at 2+ streak, +1 damage.
    • On the home map, click tree, tent, or river for a one-time first-strike boon.

    Ranbhoomi Rules & Scenarios

    Core Goal

    Defeat the enemy Leader. Strategy and positioning matter more than raw power.

    Turn Flow

    • Pandav and Kaurav hosts alternate turns (P1 then P2).
    • Against AI you always pick your army: Pandav or Kaurav (you stay on P1).
    • In local play, who is Pandav vs Kaurav is randomized each match.
    • On your turn, select one of your units and move or attack, or play one card.

    Units

    • Leader (L): critical unit; if defeated, game over.
    • Infantry (I): frontline control unit.
    • Archer (A): ranged attacker (distance 2-3).
    • Cavalry (C): fast flanking movement.
    • Elephant (E): heavy breaker.
    • Chariot (R): elite balanced striker.

    Advanced Systems

    • Formations: Line and Surround give combat bonuses.
    • Cards: Strike, Guard, and Reposition (limited charges).
    • Terrain: Forest, Hill, River, and Mud — each changes movement or combat.
    • Leader auras: Pandav morale (attack) and Kaurav intimidation (enemy move).
    • Momentum rewards repeated attacks or successful defense.
    • Random wind or battlefield mud can slightly shift damage each turn.
    • Fog of War toggle to hide enemy information at distance.
    • Mode select supports local multiplayer and AI opponent.

    Current Scenarios

    • Opening Deployment: stable default setup for both players.
    • Frontline Clash: infantry lines trade space and pressure.
    • Flank Pressure: cavalry threatens backline from side lanes.
    • Leader Hunt: focus attacks and positioning to expose enemy leader.
    • Forest Ambush: vision and terrain focused tactical map.
    • Royal Siege: high-intensity castle push and hold setup.
