    Ranbhoomi Rules & Scenarios

    Core Goal

    Defeat the enemy Leader. Strategy and positioning matter more than raw power.

    Turn Flow

    • Player 1 acts, then Player 2 acts.
    • On your turn, select one of your units.
    • Move to a valid tile or attack an enemy in range.

    Units (Phase 1)

    • Leader (L): critical unit; if defeated, game over.
    • Infantry (I): frontline control unit.
    • Archer (A): ranged attacker (distance 2-3).
    • Cavalry (C): fast flanking movement.

    Current Scenarios

    • Opening Deployment: stable default setup for both players.
    • Frontline Clash: infantry lines trade space and pressure.
    • Flank Pressure: cavalry threatens backline from side lanes.
    • Leader Hunt: focus attacks and positioning to expose enemy leader.
