    Ranbhoomi — Rules & Scenarios

    Core Goal

    Defeat the enemy Leader. Strategy and positioning matter more than raw power.

    Turn Flow

    • Each player has 10 minutes on a chess clock; only the active player's time decreases.
    • Pandav and Kaurav hosts alternate turns (P1 then P2).
    • Against AI you always pick your army: Pandav or Kaurav (you stay on P1).
    • On your turn, select one of your units and move or attack, or play one card.

    Units & Stats

    • Leader (L) — Move 1, HP 10, Dmg 3, range 1×1. Critical unit — if defeated, war is lost.
    • Infantry (I) — Move 1, HP 6, Dmg 2. Frontline cover; forms Line & Surround formations.
    • Archer (A) — Move 1, HP 4, Dmg 2. Attacks at range 2–3. Gains +1 on hills.
    • Cavalry (C) — Move 2, HP 6, Dmg 3. Fast flanking; can cross rivers.
    • Elephant (E) — Move 1, HP 9, Dmg 4. Slow but massive breaker power.
    • Chariot (R) — Move 2, HP 7, Dmg 3. Elite balanced striker; crosses rivers.

    Terrain

    • Forest — Archers deal less damage to units sheltered here.
    • Hill — Archers gain +1 damage when standing on a hill.
    • River — Only Cavalry and Chariots may occupy; melee from river is weaker.
    • Mud — Moving from a mud tile costs 1 extra movement.

    Advanced Systems

    • Formations: Line and Surround bonuses activate automatically when positioned.
    • Cards: Strike (+2 dmg), Guard (absorb), Reposition (2-tile move). 2 charges each.
    • Leader Auras: Pandav morale gives +1 attack near Pandav leader; Kaurav intimidation reduces enemy move near Kaurav leader.
    • Momentum: Chain attacks or defend successfully to build streak — at 2+ gain +1 damage.
    • Random Events: Wind, Agni's spark, healing dew, and more trigger each turn.
    • Fog of War: Toggle to hide distant enemy information.

    Scenarios

    • Opening Deployment: Stable default setup for balanced learning.
    • Frontline Clash: Infantry lines trade space and pressure from turn one.
    • Flank Pressure: Cavalry threatens backline from wide side lanes.
    • Leader Hunt: Precision attacks and tight choke points.
    • Forest Ambush: Vision and terrain-focused tactical map.
    • Royal Siege: High-intensity castle push and hold setup.
